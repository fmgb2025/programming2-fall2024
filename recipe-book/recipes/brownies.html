Description

Is it a rainy day? Do you have no will to get out of bed? well these Brownies with crispy edges, fudgy middles, and rich chocolate flavors, will disappear in no time!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/8 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 5 tablespoons of butter
  • 1 1/4 cups granulated sugar
  • 3/4 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 large eggs and 1 egg yolk.
  • 3/4 cups Chocolate Chips
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 1/3 cup vegetable oil

Steps

  1. Pre-heat oven to 325 degrees farenheit. Line an 8 by 8-inch metal pan.
  2. Microwave butter and sugar.
  3. Whisk in eggs, egg yolk, vanilla, oil, and cocoa powder to butter and sugar mixture.
  4. With a rubber spatula, stir in the flour, cornstarch, baking soda, and salt until combined.
  5. Stir in Chocolate Chips.
  6. Spread the brownie batter evenly into the prepared pan.
  7. Place in the oven and bake for 30 minutes.
  8. Enjoy!
