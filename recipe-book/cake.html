Vanilla Cake

Description

Uh oh! You forgot that it was your turn to bring the cake this year and you only have a day to make something? Well then this is the recipe page for you!

Ingredients

  • 2 2/3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
  • 3 eggs
  • 3/4 cup milk
  • 3/4 cup vegetable oil

Steps

  1. Pre-heat oven to 350 degrees farenheit.
  2. Grease a 9-inch cake tin with cooking spray and line with parchment paper
  3. Combine flour, baking powder, vanilla extract, and salt in a large bowl.
  4. Add eggs, milk and vegetable oil into the dry mixture.
  5. Pour into prepared pan.
  6. Bkae for 1 hour.
  7. Let cool on wire rack
  8. Enjoy!
