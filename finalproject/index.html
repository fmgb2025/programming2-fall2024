ABOUT ISABELLA

Isabella Gomez-Barrientos is a Chicago based actress, singer, and storyteller. A recent graduate of the University at Buffalo., she earned her B.F.A. in Music Theatre with minors in Education & Dance this past May. Isabella is constantly working to tell the stories of other little brown girls like herself, hoping to be the representation that she lacked as a child.

When not onstage, Isabella can be found crocheting a new crop top for a friend, cuddling with her two dogs (Olivia & Fraser), or playing Disney’s Dreamlight Valley and sipping on her latest obsession, an iced matcha latte.
